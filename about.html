Where is the best place for the students of Stony Brook University to eat? Where do they like to go the most for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, and why?

This website is the proof of concept for our group's Digital Arts: Web Design and Culture pitch - a place where the students of Stony Brook University can find out where the best food is on-campus from their peers. Due to time constraints placed on us by deadlines, we chose to focus on the eateries that were on-campus, but should we continue with this project, we would like to expand our range further into the town, as well.
